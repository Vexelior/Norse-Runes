You can use them to help guide you through problems or questions you may have. There are 24 runes in a set and each one has a different meaning. There are different ways to interpret the runes, but I like to use the traditional way. You can use the runes in a spread or just pull one out of the bag when you need a little guidance.

I like to pull one out of the bag each morning to see what the day has in store for me. I also like to use them when I have a question or problem I need help with. I find that they are very accurate and helpful. I hope you enjoy using them as much as I do!
